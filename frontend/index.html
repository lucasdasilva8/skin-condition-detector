What is SkinScan?

SkinScan is an educational screening tool that uses an ensemble of machine learning models trained on thousands of dermatology images. Upload a close-up photo of a skin area and receive an identification with information you can read before seeing a doctor.

Not a medical diagnosis. Results are for learning and awareness only. Always consult a dermatologist for any concerning or changing skin findings.

How to use it

  1. 1
    Take a clear photo

    Close-up, well-lit image of one skin area. JPEG or PNG, max 10 MB.

  2. 2
    Upload and analyze

    Three models compare your image against 22 common condition categories and combine their scores.

  3. 3
    Read the results

    Get an explanation, signs to watch for, dermatologist recommendations, and sources.
